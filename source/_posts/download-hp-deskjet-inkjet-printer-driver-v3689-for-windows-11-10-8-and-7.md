![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/1.jpg)

**2)** Type**HP DeskJet 2652** in the search box, and click**Submit** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/1-2-1.jpg)
