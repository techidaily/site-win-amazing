9. Click **Agree to the EULA**  **and Download** .  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/img_5b8613f9ec314.jpg)

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/img_5b861455df55e.jpg)
